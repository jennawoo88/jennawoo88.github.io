The Royal Family



King of Dale, Bowman, Dragon Slayer

Bard

The founder and first king of the New Kingdom of Dale; was once known as a bowman of Laketown, now known as the Dragon Slayer of Smaug. Descendant of Girion of Dale. A father of three children, two girls and one boy.


"I let myself imagine this city restored. We would take what had been destroyed and rebuild it. We would wash away this sadness. And the streets would once again be filled with life. Full of hope." - Bard, King of Dale
